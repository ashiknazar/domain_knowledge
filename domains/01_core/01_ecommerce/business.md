# Business Understanding

#### What is the goal
- Sell products and maximize revenue while improving customer experience.

#### Core KPIs
- Revenue
  - Total money generated
- Conversion Rate
  - % of users who make a purchase
- Cart Abandonment Rate
  - Users who add to cart but don’t buy
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
  - Total revenue from a customer over time
- Retention Rate
  - How many users come back
- Churn Rate
  - Users who stop buying
- Customer Funnel
```bash
User Journey:

Visit → View Product → Add to Cart → Checkout → Purchase
```

  - Each step drops users → this is where analysis happens
